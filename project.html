  OUR PROJECTS  

" With currently 36 project under our sponsorships, Global Nature Organisation has been a hub for campaigns that are dedicated on conserving, preserving, and restoring the ecosystems for the last decade. "

1000 Tap Water Project

1000 Fountains is focused on eliminating the demand for single-use plastic water bottles by building a network of 1000 drinking fountains throughout hilly region of Nepal.


Dadeldhura Sanitation Programme

This project was done last year and it helped village in rural area to help them build portable toilets in their house and surroundings. This programme decreased number of water borne disease cases significantly.


Clean Bagmati Project

Clean Bagmati Project, a project by Global Nature Organization, made mass awareness and focused on cleaning Bagmati, which is the main river of Kathmandu. This project reduced the waste thrown into Bagmati river each year by 10 Metric Ton.


Animal Rescue

There are lot of cases of wildfire each year throughout the Nepal and biodiversity is heavily affected by it. Many animals are forced to come into human settlement due to this wildfire. This project helped to capture them and move them back to dense forests.


Green Project

Green Project is an afforestation programme that had a goal of planting 100k trees throughout the places of Nepal. It is still an ongoing project and so far 80k plants have been planted.


Sanitation Project

This project was entirely was due to help of volunteers. It focused on cleaning the areas of UNESCO Heritage sites inside Kathmandu valley. This project took place for 7 days with the help of several volunteers.


Wild animals Treatment

An innovative idea of collecting the data and taking a survey of wildlife in Chitwan National Park. This project helps the sick animals and also have a record of their species which again helpts to increase their population.
